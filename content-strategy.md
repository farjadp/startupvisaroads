## CONTENT STRATEGY NOTES

### For AEO (Answer Engine Optimization):
- Create **structured FAQ sections** targeting Tier 3 (how/what/why) queries.
- Use **listicles**: "5 Common Visa Rejection Reasons," "7 Fastest Visas to Canada".
- Implement **FAQPage schema** where appropriate in JSON.

### For SEO Authority:
- Link related niche topics to country hub pages.
- Focus on comprehensive pillar content for SUV and PNP programs.

### For AIO (Artificial Intelligence Optimization):
- Deliver high information-density summaries at the top of pages for LLM scrapers.
- Use bullet points, bold definitions, and tabular data layouts.
